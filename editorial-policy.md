      <h2>Editorial policy</h2>
      <p>DestinationGuides.ca is designed as a planning reference. We prefer clarity over hype and avoid false precision.</p>
      <div class="callouts">
        <div class="callout">
          <div class="calloutTitle">Verification rule</div>
          <p>Hours, ticketing rules, prices, schedules, and entry requirements change. We mark these as <strong>Verify</strong> and expect readers to confirm on official sources close to travel.</p>
        </div>
        <div class="callout">
          <div class="calloutTitle">Canadian lens</div>
          <p>When it matters (e.g., documents, payment assumptions, connectivity), we add notes relevant to Canadian travelers without turning the guide into a niche site.</p>
        </div>
      </div>
