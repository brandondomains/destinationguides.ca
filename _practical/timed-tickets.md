## Overview
A structured reference to reduce friction while planning.

## What Changes Most
Hours, pricing, reservation systems, and transport schedules.

## What Stays Stable
Geography, walking distances, and fatigue patterns.

## Planning Principle
Reduce uncertainty first. Optimize after constraints are clear.
