## Overview
Plan by regions and transport corridors, not by a highlights list.

## Entry Basics
Verify passport validity, visa rules, and transit requirements (official sources).

## Regional Pairing Strategy
Limit to 1–2 major regions per week. Choose regions connected by high‑frequency transport.

## Transport Logic
Use dominant corridors (rail lines, major highways, short flight hops). Avoid zig‑zag routes.

## Budget Model
Accommodation + transport are primary drivers. Add 15% buffer for peak periods.

## Common Mistakes
Over‑stacking cities, underestimating transfer fatigue, ignoring reservation realities.
