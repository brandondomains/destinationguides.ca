## Overview
Plan by corridor logic and base count, not by highlight stacking.

## Regional pairing
Choose 1–2 regions per week. Optimize for fewer transfers.

## Transport reality
Buy speed with rail/air where it prevents full-day fatigue; avoid zig-zag itineraries.

## Reservation reality
Anchors may require timed tickets. Verify rules close to travel.
