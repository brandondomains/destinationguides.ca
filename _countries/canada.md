## Overview
Plan by region clusters and dominant transport corridors.

## Regional Logic
Limit to 1–2 major regions per week.

## Transport Reality
Prioritize rail corridors or short-haul flights over zig‑zag routes.

## Planning Mistakes
Overstacking cities and underestimating transfer fatigue.
